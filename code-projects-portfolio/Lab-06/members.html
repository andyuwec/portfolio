Tyler Joseph

Tyler Joseph is the lead guitar and vocalist for twenty one pilots, and the original founder of the band. Born in Columbus Ohio, he is 35, and married to Jenna Black with three children. He has been on all seven albums and released countless singles with the band.

Josh Dun

Josh Dun, also born in Columbus Ohio is 36 years old. He originally joined the band after former drummer Chris Salih introduced him to Tyler Joseph, quitting his job at guitar center to join the group. Currently he is married to actress Debby Ryan, known for her roles in Jessie and The Suite Life on Deck. They have no children.

Chris Salih - 2009-2011

Chris Salih, born in texas, was the drummer for the band in 2009-2011. Little is known about him, and he keeps a low online profile. He went on to focus on work and wished his farewells to the growing fanbase.

Nick Thomas - 2009-2011

Nick Thomas, born in Columbus Ohio, was a close friend of Joseph growing up, meeting eachother for the first time in elementary school. Thomas was the bassist of the band from 2009-2011, and also has almost no online presence anymore. He left the group to pursue schooling and is still living Ohio to this day.
