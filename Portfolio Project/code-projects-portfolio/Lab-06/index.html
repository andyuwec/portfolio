What is Twenty One Pilots?

Twenty One Pilots is an indie rock band started in 2009, in Columbus Ohio. The origional group consisted of the lead vocalist Tyler Joseph, Nick Thomas, Chris Salih. After the release of their first two albums Twenty One Pilots (also known as the self titled album) and Regional at Best Thomas and Salih left the band, andthat is when Josh Dun joined on as the drummer for the now duo. Since these two left the band Joseph and Dun have signed to a label (Fueled by Ramen), and released another 5 albums and some singles for different movies and such, the most popular being Heathens For the movie Suicide Squad. currently the band boasts 31 million monthly listeners on spotify and are in the middle of a world tour for their newest album Clancy.
